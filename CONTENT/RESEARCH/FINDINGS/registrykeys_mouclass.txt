Registry Keys Accessed by MOUCLASS (Mouse Class)

\Registry\Machine\SYSTEM\ControlSet001\Services\mouclass\PARAMETERS : PointerDeviceBaseName
\Registry\Machine\SYSTEM\ControlSet001\Services\mouclass\PARAMETERS : VerboseOn
\Registry\Machine\SYSTEM\ControlSet001\Services\mouclass\PARAMETERS : WppRecorder_PerBufferMaxBytes
